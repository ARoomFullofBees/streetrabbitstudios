Apocalypse Gardens
(Zone 5 Edible and Medicinal Xeriscapes)


Xeriscaped yards as a replacement for lawns are smart, beautiful, and useful. Many plants and shrubs native to Western Montana produce food and medicine with little to no irrigation. Strategically placing these plants in areas where you want to preserve water, is a wise option. Plus, it will come in handy when disaster strikes and civilization breaks down. Sure traditonal crops like corn, squash, and beans are ideal in survival situations, but what happens when you can't use city water to irrigate? Native plants and shrubs will continue to thrive even after Captain Tripps takes hold or amidst throngs of zombies.
